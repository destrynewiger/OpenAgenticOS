## Persona Angles

VP Engineering:
Standardize incident ownership across teams, reduce manual coordination, and keep customer-facing updates consistent.

SRE Manager:
Reduce on-call noise, make response handoffs clearer, and tighten post-incident follow-through.

Software Engineer:
Make it easier to know who owns the incident, where updates live, and what follow-up work remains.

## Good CTAs

Who owns incident response process there?
Worth comparing how teams handle the work after the first alert fires?
Open to pressure-testing one recent incident workflow?

## Avoid

Do not claim they use a tool unless the data says so.
Do not invent outage history.
Do not mention customer names unless they are provided.
Do not over-explain the product.

## Positioning

Coordinate incident response, on-call handoffs, stakeholder updates, automation, and retrospectives without replacing every existing tool.

## Proof Points

Preferred wording:
Teams usually need help with the response work after the first alert: ownership, communication, follow-up, and learning.
